# Speaker level

The [[audio-signal|audio signal]] output from signal amplifiers are at **speaker level**.

- strongest [[audio-signal|audio signal]] level
- intended for [[loudspeakers|loudspeaker]] [[driver-audio|drivers]] only
- approx. 10-100 V
- varies by amplifier
