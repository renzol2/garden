# Filter ripple

**Ripples** are fluctuations in a [[filter]]'s [[filter-response-curve|response curve]].

- Elliptic and Chebyshev-based filters have constant ripple across their pass bands
- Bessel and Butterworth derived filters have no ripple in their pass band responses
