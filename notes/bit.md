# Bit

The **bit** is the most basic unit of information in the digital realm. It represents a logical state of one of two values:

- `1` or `0`
- `true`/`false`
- `yes`/`no`
- `+`/`-`
- `on`/`off`

"Bit" is a contraction of "binary digit"
